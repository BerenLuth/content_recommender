Remember the Xbox Onesie? How could you forget. Microsoft's Australian marketing team created a real Xbox Onesie with enlarged pockets for Xbox controllers, and rollable legs and arms for all seasons. Unfortunately, or fortunately, Microsoft never sold the Xbox Onesies to the general public, but you can now get one for your virtual self.
